About Photo Editing

A photo editor professional is responsible for editing and retouching photographs for various mediums using specialized software and techniques to enhance the visual appeal of images. They work closely with photographers and clients to ensure the final product meets the creative vision and technical requirements. They possess a keen eye for detail, understanding of composition, color theory and visual storytelling and deep understanding of technical aspects of photography.
